About

Founding

Gargoyle Software was founded in 2011 after David Kay left Google in search of a more rewarding existence.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to create more value than we capture, growing our customers as we grow our own expertise.

Immutable Values

These are the core values that Gargoyle holds most dear:

Truth above all

Simply put, no man is greater than the truth. If this means that we tell you that we aren't the best firm for the job, that's what must be done.

No Kobayashi Maru

There is no situation that can't be made better. It might take hard work, original thinking, or a minor miracle, but there's never reason to lose hope.

Be broad AND deep

It's easy to know one subject well. It's easy to give the synopsis on a range of topics. What's truly special is to master multiple disciplines.

Do it better each time

Life is a long haul. You never get it perfect on the first pass, but if you can stick to it and make daily adjustments, you'll approach perfection with every passing day.

Love your tools

A tools are a craftsman's lifeblood. Invest in them, make love to them, and they will treat you better for it.
