Case Study: Camra

Summary:

Make short films on your phone

Needs:

When Camra approached us, they were still known as MyBlockNYC, a website that showcased video on every city block of NYC.

They spoke to us about creating a cutting-edge, next-generation video app.

Of of their co-founders was a filmmaker and had a vision for a seamless shooting & editing experience, where one would shoot as they would edit and edit as they would shoot.

Could we bring this to life?

Delivered:

The resulting app is an app not unlike Instagram, where users can log in, follow one another, create videos of their own, etc.

We include all the normal social features such as feeds, lists, location-awareness, etc.

Our proudest technical feature is the real-time digital zoom powered by GPU acceleration. We beleive this is the first implementation on any app on iOS to do this.

(Note that the above screenshots are from an early version.)

Result:

Their private beta users love the app, but the founders are debating whether to attpempt to raise a round of funding or license their software to media companies.
