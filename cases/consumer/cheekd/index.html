Case Study: Cheek'd

Summary:

Dating App

Needs:

Going on TV for shark tank

Cheek'd had filmed their episode of Shark Tank in the fall, but didn't know whether they would make the final cut and air on TV.

Three weeks before the air date, they heard back.

After thinking about it for a day or two, they gave us a call, saying "we want to launch an iPhone app and an Android just in time for the show. We have a partially-done iPhone app but no Android app."

In order to meet the tight deadline, we started that same day.

Delivered:

We delivered a both apps in less than 3 weeks.

In order to get this done, we:

  • Brought in 6 men to supplement their team
  • Led and managed an 11-man team
  • Worked with & built upon existing designs
  • Troubleshot their existing API

Result:

The Android app went live just in time for the show, but the iPhone app was rejected on grounds of no way of flagging pornographic user photos.

After some cleanup, the apps are in both stores and Cheek'd is now enjoying some media buzz and interest from investors.
