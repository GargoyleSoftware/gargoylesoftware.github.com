Case Study: EnHatch

Summary:

High tech sales w/ chat, metrics, and 3D

Needs:

EnHatch started with no IT system and no existing business model, but had deep mechanical and medical engineering experience and the vision on how they would revolutionize industrial sales. Using a prior startup exit to finance their venture, they were ready to swing hard.

Delivered:

Getting involved as they made the transition from medical device engineering to software, we became intimately acquainted with their industry, even flying out to the AAOS orthopedic summit in San Francisco.

After prototyping two different concepts, we settled on an analytics-driven, content-focused sales and marketing app on the iPad with full suport for 3D, movies, PDFs.

Once the application was up and running at full bore, we fully trained their CTO to take over development from us.

Result:

They are now raising a round of funding and have closed sales from large corporations including GE Transportation.

Peter Verrillo

CEO, EnHatch

"Gargoyle quickly got us to a real product and trained our CTO to take the reins. Can't ask for much more than that."
