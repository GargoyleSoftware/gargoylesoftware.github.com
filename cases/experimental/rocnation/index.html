Case Study: Roc Nation

Summary:

Album app for Jay-Z

Needs:

Roc Nation, Jay-Z's record label, put out an RFP for an album app to celebrate one of their top artists' new releases.

Given very little information, we were given one week to submit a project proposal for what we would build for them, if given the opportunity.

Delivered:

We did our best to guess which artist they were speaking of and what they were looking for.

Based on some vague clues, we guessed it was Jay-Z and decided to design & build the app that we WOULD have built for his previous album, The Blueprint 3.

Given only a few days to turn around the project, we produced a fully-working prototype app and a proposal document.

We included features such as ordering merchandise & concert tickets, as well as music videos, video interviews and behind-the-scenes footage.

However, the most exciting feature is the "karaoke mode" where the lyrics scroll in time with the music. Not only that, "lyric annotations" (powered by RapGenius.com) appear side-by-side so that users can more deeply understand the meaning of the music!

Result:

The good news: turns our the artist was Jay-Z, as we had guessed.

The bad news: Roc Nation ended up partnering with Samsung, instead.

Ordinarily, we'd be crushed. However, Samsung paid a healthy $5MM to Roc Nation, where we had been planning to CHARGE Roc Nation.
