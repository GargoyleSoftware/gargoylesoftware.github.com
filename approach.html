Approach

"All work is an act of philosophy."

Total Alignment

At Gargoyle, results are paramount. To achieve the best results, we strive to ensure we are aligned with you at every step in the process.

To do this, we take three important steps:

1. Results-based Pricing

Unlike other firms, we don't like charging by the hour. Simple butt-in-chair time doesn't always get the job done.

Instead, we prefer to be paid for real results.

Thus, we'll work with you either on a deliverables basis or a business results basis. For example, let's say we are replacing a pen & paper inventory management system.

If pricing by deliverables, we could charge a total fee of $100,000, where we release a new version every month for four months and collect $25,000 on each new release.

If pricing by business results, we may charge 30% of the labor savings by moving to digital, plus a small kick-off fee of $20,000.

In the latter case, we are incented to achieve the best possible results for you.

2. Stand-in

To make sure that our team understands your needs perfectly, we'll grant you an account manager whose chief responsibility is to serve as a stand-in stakeholder for all discussions relevant to your project.

In essence, The account manager's job is to be stern with our team so you don't have to.

3. Regular Communication

To keep in sync during the process, you can expect an updated app in your hand every week along with a phone call from your account manager to discuss the state of the project.

We encourage the practice of active testing in the field. Since we track all app crashes and bugs remotely, we'll be able to diagnose problems you discover in no time.

Also, if you hold internal design meetings, we'd be happy to attend them.
